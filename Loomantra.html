The LOOMANTRA Creation Process

Custom style in three simple, intuitive steps.

1. The Co-Design Session

Use our interactive 3D studio to select your garment type, choose sustainable fabrics, and define every detail—from pocket style to hem length.

1
2

2. Digital Fit Mapping

Upload simple photos or input your measurements. Our AI refines the pattern, and a LOOMANTRA designer confirms the fit, ensuring zero guesswork.

3. Ethical Crafting & Delivery

Our skilled artisans cut and sew your unique piece with minimal material wastage. It's then delivered in compostable packaging, ready to be worn for years.
